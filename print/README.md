# ClearNest — Print Pack

Professional-grade print materials in the same liquid-glass aesthetic as
the website. All files are self-contained HTML — **no design software
needed.** Open in any browser → ⌘P / Ctrl+P → "Save as PDF" or print
direct.

## Files

| File | Size | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `poster.html` | 8.5 × 11 in (US Letter) | Coffee-shop bulletin boards, gyms, libraries, laundromats, apartment lobbies |
| `business-cards.html` | 10 cards on Letter (front + back) | Hand out at every job, leave at counters, exchange with neighbors |
| `door-hanger.html` | 4.25 × 11 in (Letter, left half) | Canvass the neighbors after every job |

## Print instructions

1. **Open the HTML file** in Chrome, Safari, or Firefox.
2. **Cmd+P** (Mac) / **Ctrl+P** (Windows).
3. In the print dialog:
   - **Destination:** Save as PDF *(or pick your printer)*
   - **Paper size:** US Letter
   - **Margins:** None *(critical — the designs already include their own margins)*
   - **Scale:** 100% *(do NOT use "Fit to page" — it'll distort the dimensions)*
   - **Background graphics:** ON *(turn this on or you'll lose all the soft gradients and color)*
4. Save → send to your printer, or upload the PDF to a print shop (FedEx,
   Office Depot, Staples, Vistaprint).

### Paper recommendations
- **Poster:** matte presentation paper, 80–100 lb cover.
- **Business cards:** matte card stock, 14–16 pt. (Or print on regular
  paper to prototype, then send the PDF to a print shop for production.)
- **Door hanger:** 100 lb card stock so it survives weather + handling.

### After printing
- **Poster:** pin to community boards.
- **Business cards:** trim along the dashed guides. (Letter sheet holds 10
  cards; cut into 2 columns × 5 rows.) If double-siding at home, set "long-edge"
  binding so the back aligns with the front.
- **Door hanger:** trim the rectangle, cut out the dashed oval at the top
  for the doorknob.

## What the QR codes point to

All QR codes on these prints link to **`https://clearnest.services`** — your
booking page. They're rendered live at print time from a free public API
(`api.qrserver.com`), so the printed scan always works.

If you'd ever prefer a fully offline / branded QR (e.g. with the
ClearNest logo in the middle, or pointing to a specific landing page like
`/founding-offer`), say the word — easy upgrade.

## Design notes — why these look the way they do

- **One mark, one story.** A peaked roof above a curved nest cradle, with
  a gold spark inside — that's the *Clear Nest*. It's the same mark in
  the website nav, favicon, and these prints.
- **Premium paper aesthetic.** Soft `#f6f6f1` cream background + subtle
  brand-teal and gold ambient gradients = the liquid-glass language of
  the site, carried into print.
- **No bucket-of-supplies clipart.** Most cleaning marketing reaches for
  the same generic mop-and-bubbles imagery. ClearNest is positioned as a
  premium, trust-led service — the prints reflect that.
- **Truth-in-advertising.** Every claim on these prints is currently true:
  insured & bonded, background-checked W-2 cleaners, eco/pet-safe products,
  pay after the clean. **The 20% off founding offer is capped at 10 homes
  — once you've delivered to 10, update or remove the offer copy.** Keep
  the prints honest as the business grows.
