## Strategy (read this first — 60 seconds)

You're brand-new with zero reviews. The fastest free path to first customers:

1. **Warm beats cold.** Tell people you know + post in local community channels
   (Nextdoor, neighborhood/city Facebook groups). People hire a *person*, not a logo.
2. **Run a "Founding Customer" offer** that trades a small discount for the one thing
   money can't buy yet: **honest reviews + before/after photos.** Those reviews are
   what make your Google Business Profile rank — which is then a free lead machine.

**Three rules I kept (keep them too):**
- ✅ **Only true claims.** Insured & bonded · pay-after-service · eco & family/pet-safe ·
  background-checked W-2 cleaners. All real. **Never fake a review, rating, or "5 stars."**
- ✅ **Capacity-aware.** You can handle ~6–15 jobs/week, so the founding offer is capped
  at **10 homes**. Never generate more demand than you can wow — a blown first job becomes
  a bad first review.
- ✅ **Lead with "pay after the clean."** For a brand with no reviews, removing the
  upfront risk is your single most disarming line. Put it everywhere.
